Trade With Exness App

Beginning the Exness Login process

Follow the instructions below to log into your account:

  • Go to the official website
  • Click Sign In in the top right corner
  • Enter your email and password
  • Click Continue
  • Complete Two Factor Authentication (if you have this enabled)

Once you are logged in, you will be taken to your Personal Area, which is where you can manage your trades, funds, and configuration. Be sure to check that there is a secure "https://" at the beginning of the page before entering your credentials.

Changing Base Currency

Your base currency will determine how balances and reports are shown. Selecting a base currency entail:

  • Click My Accounts in your Personal Area
  • Click on the settings of the account you wish to amend
  • Select Change Base Currency
  • Select from the base currency options provided
  • Confirm

Note: The base currency option can only be changed if the account is new and there is no trading activity.

Changing the Terminal

Want to change the way you trade? After completing your Exness Login, do as follows:

  • Enter your Personal Area
  • Select the trading account
  • Click Settings and Change Terminal
  • Select from MetaTrader 4, MetaTrader 5, or the Exness Web Terminal
  • Click Save and continue trading your preferred terminal
